About Me

Hello! My name is Shijie (Brandon) Bian, a senior undergraduate student at UCLA, majoring in Mathematics of Computation and minoring in Statistics.

Education: During my undergraduate study, I have gained an overall GPA of 3.86/4.0 and a major GPA of 3.89/4.0. Furthermore, I am proficient in designing complicated programs using Python, R, C, and C++. I am also skilled at developing machine learning models using PyTorch, TensorFlow, Keras, Scikit-learn, and NetworkX.

Research Interests: I am interested in research areas related to Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Knowledge Engineering, Data Mining, and their applications. I wish to further my study and research in academic realms related to Artificial Intelligence and apply my knowledge of Mathematics, Computer Science, and Statistics as well.

Research Experience: I have conducted numerous research internships and projects related to Machine Learning during my undergraduate studies. Specifically, I participated as the core researcher in the "Smart Connected Worker" project directed by UC Irvine, CSU Northridge, and funded by the U.S. Department of Energy; the "AI-assisted Knowledge Graph Design" project directed by NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL) with Autodesk Inc; "An Optimizing Compiler for Deep Learning" project at The Center for Vision, Cognition, Learning, and Autonomy of UCLA.
